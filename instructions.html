Fluxable: Design and Fabrication of Interactive Objects Using Inductive Sensing

  • Welcome to this study! Your participation is very important to our research.

  • Our research team developed a new sensing technique to convert 3D-printed objects into interactive devices.

  • The purpose of this study is to investigate if this new technique is accurate, reliable, and robust across different individuals and application contexts.

  • Before the experiment actually begins, please make sure that you understand the goals and the whole process of the experiment.

  • During the experiment, please make sure your cellphone is off in case of any external interference and electromagnetic effects caused by cell phones.

  • Please follow the instructions from the research staff, and conduct movements according to what the screen shows you.

  • After this study finishes, you will get a $10 gift card to thank you for your participation.

  • Please feel free to let any one of us know if you have any problems with your experiment or other help.
